Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

What types of steel products do you manufacture?
We manufacture a wide range of steel products, including reinforced steel bars, sheets, pipes, and custom steel solutions tailored to your specific needs.
How can I place an order?
You can place an order by contacting us directly via our website, phone, or email. Simply provide your specifications, and we’ll guide you through the process.
Do you provide 24/7 support?
Yes, our team is available 24/7 to assist with inquiries, orders, and any concerns you may have.
Can I visit your facility?
Absolutely! We welcome visits to our facility. Please contact us in advance to schedule a tour.
How can I request a quote?
You can request a quote through our website or by contacting us directly. Provide your project details, and we’ll respond promptly.
What is the minimum order quantity?
We cater to orders of all sizes, whether small or large. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and other secure payment options. Contact us for more details.
Can you handle bulk orders?
Yes, we have the capacity to handle bulk orders for large-scale projects. Contact us for bulk pricing.
